    • Many patients are not satisfied with how doctors communicate with them.
    • Patients want to be more involved in planning their care and making decisions about their treatment
    • It is often difficult for patients to understand all the instructions from their doctors, especially when they receive too much information at on time.
    • This is especially true for patients with chronic diseases such as diabetes where treatment is complicated and lifelong.
